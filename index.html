About Ashraf Bidaki

Ashraf Bidaki is a researcher focused on ideas related to dialogue, peace, human dignity, and understanding across cultures. This personal website serves as a place to share research interests, intellectual projects, and long-term visions for a more thoughtful and humane world.

Ideas / Projects

Dialogue Across Cultures

One of the main interests of Ashraf Bidaki is the role of dialogue in reducing misunderstanding between people, cultures, and communities. This project explores how meaningful conversation can build trust, understanding, and peace.

Peace and Human Understanding

Ashraf Bidaki studies ideas related to peace, coexistence, and the shared dignity of human beings. This work reflects on how societies can move away from hostility and toward respect, empathy, and thoughtful engagement.
